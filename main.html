How to Play



1) Wait for the 2nd player to enter there name in a similiar fashion.

2) Once all players are ready to play the computer will tell you who's turn it is.

3) The player will be allowed to click 2 cards. If both cards match that player will be given another chance to match another 2 cards.

4) If cards do not match the next player will have a chance to match the cards.

5) Player who has matched the most cards will wins

Enjoy and good luck!!!
